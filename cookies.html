1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to improve user experience by remembering your preferences and providing customized content.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: These are required for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled. They include login authentication cookies and session cookies.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how users interact with Writix by tracking their behavior on the site. They are used to improve the performance of the site.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and customize the user experience accordingly.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies track your browsing habits to show relevant advertisements and offers.
