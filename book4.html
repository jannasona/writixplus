FirstLast

By Aisha Raman

What if your first love was also your last goodbye?

"FirstLast" is a poetic rollercoaster of two souls destined to meet — not at the beginning, but at the end. Through heartbeats and heartbreaks, it unravels a journey of finding the right person at the wrong time, again and again.

It’s not a question of whether love was real. It’s whether it ever had a chance to stay.
