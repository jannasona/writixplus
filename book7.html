NeverbeYours

The Battle Continues – Sequel to Neverbe

In the aftermath of war and vows that once felt forced, Prince Kaelen and Princess Arina now rule side by side. But peace is fragile. Shadows of betrayal, political deception, and a new enemy rising from within their own kingdoms threaten everything they’ve built together.

As love deepens between the once-enemies, so do the challenges of trust, loyalty, and sacrifice. Are their hearts strong enough to survive a second war? Or will the past claim them once again?

"NeverbeYours" is an emotional continuation of an unlikely love story forged in fire and sealed by fate.
