Starelife

By Elan Creswell

In a world that rushes ahead, he chose to stare back.

"Starelife" follows the directive journey of a young boy trapped between the dreams in his head and the pressure of the world outside. He’s quiet, observant, and painfully aware of how different he is. But he learns that sometimes, just watching life teaches you how to live it.

A coming-of-age tale that’s tender, raw, and full of poetic silence, this story is about listening to your inner voice when everyone else is shouting.
