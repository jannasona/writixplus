Megala

By Aru Das

She wasn’t born to be saved. She was born to survive.

"Megala" is the fierce and fearless story of a girl who rises from pain to power. With a heart scarred by betrayal and eyes that burn with fire, she reclaims every part of herself the world tried to erase.

This is not a fairytale. It’s a saga of war, womanhood, and the will to become something more than what you’re told.
