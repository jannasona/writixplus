Lovebye

By Myra Noelle

In every lifetime, they find each other. Different names. Different faces. Same souls.

"Lovebye" is a poetic tragedy of two fated hearts who are always born to love… but never to last. Every life begins with a spark and ends with a goodbye. Every goodbye breaks a little deeper.

This is not a story about a happy ending. It’s about why they never got one — and why their love still echoes through time.
